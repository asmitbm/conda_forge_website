# conda-forge Styleguide

A conda-forge brand guide is an all-encompassing document that lays out the principles and norms
for consistently displaying and portraying the conda-forge brand. It acts as a crucial source of
information for staff, collaborators, and outside firms, guaranteeing that the conda-forge brand
is consistently, precisely, and appropriately showcased while staying aligned with the brand's
principles and communication.

## Typography

Below are various typography elements. The header font for [conda-forge.org](https://conda-forge.org) is
[Montserrat](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Montserrat) and the body font is
[Inter](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter). It looks serious and neat, not too loud.
It has all the letters for many languages and comes in different styles to always work well.
